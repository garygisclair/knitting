Instructions — Adult Size 7 (88 stitches)

1 Cast On: Using long-tail cast on, cast on 88 stitches. Join in the round, being careful not to twist. Place marker at beginning of round.
2 Ribbing: Work K1, P1 rib for 9 rounds (~1.5″ / 3.8 cm).
3 Body: Switch to stockinette stitch (knit every round). Continue until piece measures 6.5″ / 16.5 cm from cast-on edge (fitted) or 8″ / 20.3 cm (slouchy).
4 Crown Decreases: See detailed crown schedule below.
5 Finishing: Cut yarn leaving 6″ tail. Thread through remaining 10 stitches with tapestry needle, pull tight, and weave in ends.

Crown Decrease Schedule — Size 7 Adult (88 sts, 10 wedges)

After body is complete, the crown shaping uses 10 decrease wedges spaced evenly around the hat. Each decrease round removes 10 stitches. A plain knit round follows each decrease round.

Decrease Math
Start: 88 stitches ÷ 10 wedges = pre-decrease adjustment needed
Adjust down to 80 stitches (divisible by 10 × 8)
Then: *K2tog, K[N]* × 10, removing 10 sts per decrease round
Setup 1 *K9, K2tog* × 8 — preliminary decrease 80 sts
Setup 2 Knit even 80 sts
Rnd 1 *K2tog, K6* × 10 70 sts
Rnd 2 Knit even 70 sts
Rnd 3 *K2tog, K5* × 10 60 sts
Rnd 4 Knit even 60 sts
Rnd 5 *K2tog, K4* × 10 50 sts
Rnd 6 Knit even 50 sts
Rnd 7 *K2tog, K3* × 10 40 sts
Rnd 8 Knit even 40 sts
Rnd 9 *K2tog, K2* × 10 30 sts
Rnd 10 Knit even 30 sts
Rnd 11 *K2tog, K1* × 10 20 sts
Rnd 12 Knit even 20 sts
Rnd 13 *K2tog* × 10 10 sts
Crown geometry: The crown spans approximately 14 rounds (including plain rounds) at 6 rnds/in = 2.33″ / 5.9 cm of crown depth. The circumference at each decrease level: 19.6″ → 17.8″ → 15.6″ → 13.3″ → 11.1″ → 8.9″ → 6.7″ → 4.4″ → 2.2″ (gather closed).
